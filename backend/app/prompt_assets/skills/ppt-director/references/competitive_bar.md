# Competitive Bar

The target quality bar is above generic AI slide generators.

## Capabilities To Match Or Exceed

- Deep document understanding before slide writing.
- Structured delivery rather than raw text-to-slide conversion.
- Template and brand adaptation without demo-content leakage.
- Editable PPTX export with web preview parity.
- Speaker notes for presenter readiness.
- Explicit evidence and quality checks.
- Slide role variation and narrative pacing.

## Quality Signals

- The first slide communicates the exact user topic.
- The deck has a recognizable narrative arc.
- The executive takeaway can be understood in under one minute.
- Slide titles form a readable storyline by themselves.
- Visuals are chosen for comprehension, not decoration.
- Recommendations are specific enough to act on.
- Source caveats and assumptions are preserved without cluttering slides.

## Failure Signals

- The deck looks like a template with swapped text.
- Slides repeat "背景/分析/总结" without claims.
- Tables or paragraphs are pasted at unreadable size.
- Key numbers lack time period, unit, or source.
- Preview differs materially from exported PPTX.
